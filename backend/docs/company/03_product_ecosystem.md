# 03 Product Ecosystem

Based on the official data retrieved from `zerostic.com` and public app stores, the Zerostic product suite includes:

## 1. AppLab
- **Description:** An education technology platform designed for aspiring developers to master Android app development.
- **Features:** It features a curated selection of video lectures and an integrated in-app IDE to facilitate mobile learning and coding directly within the application.
- **Target Audience:** Students, aspiring Android developers.

## 2. FnO Bazar (Android and Web Platform)
- **Description:** A stock market data visualization and analysis platform tailored for investors and traders. 
- **Role:** Zerostic acts as the dedicated technology partner that developed and maintains the FnO Bazar ecosystem.
- **Target Audience:** Traders, financial analysts, and retail investors.

## 3. LOA Mobile Application
- **Description:** A dedicated mobile application built for Legend Outdoor Advertising (LOA), a prominent outdoor advertising agency in India.
- **Role:** Custom software development project by Zerostic to digitize operations for this client.

## 4. Good Morning - Everyday Alarm
- **Description:** A machine-learning-powered alarm application.
- **Features:** It recommends optimal wake-up times and methods based on smart algorithms and user habits.

## 5. Zerostic Studio
- **Description:** The central B2B client portal (SaaS).
- **Features:** Project scoping, quotation generation, contract signing, tracking execution, threaded messaging, and payment proof handling.
- **Integrated AI:** Houses **JAI**, the native Zerostic AI assistant, designed to handle account management and PRD generation.
