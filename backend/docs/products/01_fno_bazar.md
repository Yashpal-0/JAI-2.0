# FnO Bazar — Product Overview

## What is FnO Bazar?

FnO Bazar is a mobile application built by Zerostic for the Indian Futures & Options (F&O) derivatives market. It provides market triggers, analytics, and tools for F&O traders.

## Platform

- **Available on:** Google Play Store (Android)
- **Type:** Mobile application
- **Market:** Indian stock market — Futures & Options (F&O) derivatives

## Core Capabilities

- **Market Triggers:** Set up alerts and triggers based on F&O market conditions
- **Telemetry & Analytics:** Track market data, position performance, and trading metrics
- **FnO Data:** Access Futures & Options data relevant to Indian markets (e.g., NIFTY, BANKNIFTY, and individual stock derivatives)

## Who Uses FnO Bazar?

- F&O traders in Indian financial markets
- Retail investors tracking derivatives
- Anyone interested in Futures & Options on Indian exchanges (NSE/BSE)
