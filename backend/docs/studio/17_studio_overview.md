# 17 Studio Overview

## What is Zerostic Studio?
Zerostic Studio is the authenticated client portal for Zerostic's B2B software development services. It acts as a centralized dashboard where clients can manage their digital transformation projects from initial scoping to final invoicing.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Unified Transparency:** Consolidates project tracking, contract signing, and financial records into a single interface.
- **Manual Automation Bridging:** Facilitates off-platform actions (like NEFT/UPI bank transfers) by providing a structured proof-upload mechanism.
- **Direct Access:** Offers both synchronous scheduling with the Zerostic team and asynchronous AI assistance via JAI.

## Target User
The target user is the "Lead Client" or project sponsor who requires high-level oversight of budgets, timelines, and legal documents, rather than granular, code-level access.

## Key Modules
- **Identity & Verification:** Mandatory government ID upload to establish trust.
- **Quotations:** Form-driven project scoping (Web, Mobile, Custom Software, UI/UX, etc.) with budget and timeline parameters.
- **Contracts & Projects:** Legal binding and subsequent status tracking.
- **Financials:** Payments (proof submission) and Invoices (PDF downloads).
- **Communications:** Threaded admin messages, video call scheduler, and AI chat (JAI).
